Tidal Coast

Explorers love to venture out to the coast and spot all sorts of creatures residing the area. Here, you can gain easy access and close-up to all your friendly rocky friends. From sea anemonies to sea snails, cherish some face-to-face moments with our famouse touch pool.

Kelp Forest

Within several thousand feet from the Web Aquarium, there is a kelp forest from the coast that is home to hundreds of species. We bring the kelp forest to you to explore what roams within the seaweed-rich environment.
